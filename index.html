Edge Quintanilla: About Me

Hi, my name is Edge Quintanilla.
I also go by Edgar.

I have a background in museum education and cultural institutions. My interests focus on digital learning experiences. Currently I manage a design education platform DiscoverDesign for Chicago Architecture Foundation.

These days I am trying to level up my life. I've been an education practitioner in museums and cultural institutions for a few years now. I've taught kids to program Arduinos, sample museum instruments, hack fruit, and get started with designing things. My hopes are to move from being a good user of digital learning tools to a good designer and developer of learning.
